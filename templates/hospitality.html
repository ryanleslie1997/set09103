Irn Bru

Irn-Bru is a Scottish carbonated soft drink, often described as "Scotland's other national drink". Introduced in 1901, the drink is produced in Westfield, Cumbernauld, North Lanarkshire, by A.G. Barr of Glasgow.

Chippys

One of the most popular types of food in Scotland is food from the chippy (Chip Shop). You can even get a fried Mars bar!

Haggis

Haggis is a very popular food in Scotland. It is often eaten with neeps (turnip) and tatties (potatoes).
